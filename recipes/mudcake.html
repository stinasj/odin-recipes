Mud cake

For the chocolate lover

This mud cake is easy to make and full of delicious chocolate. Also it has pecans in it.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 Â½ cup of butter
  • 2 cups of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 cups of chopped pecans

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease one 13x9 inch baking pan.
  2. Cream together 1/3 cup of the cocoa, white sugar, and 1 cup of the butter or margarine until light and fluffy. Add the eggs and beat well.
  3. Mix flour and chopped pecans together. Stir flour mixture into the egg mixture and stir until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared pan.
