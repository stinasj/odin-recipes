Fresh Tomato Soup

A lovely fresh tomato soup

This tomato soup recipe is simple, quick, and perfect to make when tomatoes are ripe in gardens

and farmers' markets for a delicious summertime treat. Delicious with garlic bread or a grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic. A lot.
  • Onions
  • Butter
  • Sugar and salt

Steps

  1. Boil the tomatoes, garlic and onions
  2. Add other stuff
  3. Season it
  4. Enjoy
